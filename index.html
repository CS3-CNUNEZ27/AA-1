How Do I Take Good Care of My Cats?

1. Provide suitable or recommended meals for your cats with a constant supply of fresh water everyday.

2. Make sure they have a comfortable and clean place to sleep everynight.

3. Always provide your cat with outdoor access, especially if you have a cage for your cat, it is advised to let them out for atleast a long while so they dont get stressed.

4. The environment for your cat should be safe and non-hostile.

5. Always groom your cat regularly.

6. Have a litterbox which is to be cleaned daily for your cats comfort.

7. Take the cat to the vet when there are any sign of illness.
